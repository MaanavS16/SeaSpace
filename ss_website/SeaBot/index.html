Meet Sea Space

Sea Space is the first ever ocean-centric discord bot, aimed at education and entertainment for all your oceanic needs. Sea Space can do anything you need, from finding up to date stats about the world's bodies of water to jamming out to crab raves and sea shanties.

Features

#Sea Soothe

Play's some relaxing sounds of the sea

#Sea Songs

Plays a random song relating to the sea and/or oceans

#Sea Stats {body of water}

Get up to date information about your favorite sea/ocean

#Sea Species {location}

Finds all the different species living in an area

#Sea Spot {location}

Find out where you are currently on the earth, and find out what body of water you are in

#Sea Size {Units}

Convert the massive scale of the ocean into easy to understand measurments.

#Sea Story

Learn stories about brave heroes that braved the mighty seas

#Sea Scene

Look at beautiful sea-scapes from around the world
